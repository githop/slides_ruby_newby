Integer division can be useful

How many shirts can I iron in 15 minutes?

15 minutes

4 minutes to iron a shirt

15 / 4 #=> 3
slide: 29 This could be the intended behavior, * perhaps I have 15 minutes available to iron laundered shirts and it takes me * 4 minutes to iron a shirt. How many shirts can I iron in this time? * 3 shirts. It’s safe to assume ironing a shirt ¾ of the way is not considered job well done.
