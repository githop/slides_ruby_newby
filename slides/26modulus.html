Modulus

Divide the first term by the second term

return what is left over

15 % 4 #=> 3

4 goes into 15 three times, with three left over. 4 * 3 = 12 + 3 = 15.

Modulus tends to be the least familiar to those new to programming. Modulus works as follows: divide the first term by the second term; * return what is left over.
